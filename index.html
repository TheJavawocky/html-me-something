My 'Magic: the Gathering' Decks

  • Modern Burn
  • Legacy Death & Taxes
  • Pauper Affinity
I have played Magic: the Gathering for about 6 years now. These are the decks that I have built for competitive play in that time. I primarily play in non-rotating formats, so I can continue to play with the decks I have built without having to worry about set rotation.

I play Burn in Modern. The deck is designed to be as efficient and consistant as possible, running many redundant cards that achieve the same goal; reducing the opponent to 0 life.
